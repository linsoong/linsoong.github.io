Privacy Policy for Neon Screen

Last Updated:

Neon Screen does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information or data of any kind.

Data Collection

Neon Screen collects zero user data. The app does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Store any data on your device or remotely
  • Share information with third parties
  • Transmit data over the internet
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Display advertisements
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Request any device permissions
  • Run in the background

Internet Access

Neon Screen does not require or use internet access. All functionality operates entirely offline on your device.

Third-Party Services

Neon Screen does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Neon Screen does not collect any information from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Since Neon Screen does not collect data, any policy changes will only serve to clarify our commitment to privacy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app's support channels.
